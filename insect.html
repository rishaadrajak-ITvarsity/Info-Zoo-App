INSECT HOUSE

Ever wanted to be completely surrounded by bugs, worms, grasshoppers, slugs, spiders, from slimy to hairy to soft and wriggly? Then you've come to the right place! Here, you will see the beauty of insects, learn their importance in our ecosysten and see them do some impressive things - tiny as they are.
