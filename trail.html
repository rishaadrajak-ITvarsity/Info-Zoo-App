LOST FOREST

The Monkey Trail is an elaborate obstacle course and hiking path, full of twists and turns for you to test your outdoor skills.
The Trail has different difficulties, or paths, that cater for every experience group. The challenges are always changing, so no two visits will be the same. Along the path you'll also find your monkey-cousins hanging around, showing you how it's done, but don't worry, it's completely safe.

Great News! The Monkey Trail will be opening soon, ready for you to tackle the obstacles and improve your skills.

WHERE TO FIND THEM

The Monkey Trail will be found to the east of the main entrance.
