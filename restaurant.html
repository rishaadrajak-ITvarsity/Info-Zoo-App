OUTDOOR RESTAURANT

An Outdoor Restaurant is all about enjoying a meal surrounded by nature.
The restaurant has menus for breakfast, brunch, dinner, dessert, beverages and kids, catering for specialised diets aswell - such as gluten free or vegan.
And if you're asking yourself, 'but, why outdoor?', it has been found that eating a meal in a relaxing environment allows our bodies to digest the food better! Instead of eating while texting or watching TV, which distracts our minds and inhibits our bodies from absorbing nutrients, Mindful eating helps our bodies make full use of the food we eat.

You can read more about how distraction is perceived as stress, and its impact on digestion by Clicking Here.

But it's not just for health reasons. The restaurant setting also allows for intimate gathering that deepen relations and most importantly exotic foods, that are delicious and nutritious, prepared by world-class chefs in various styles of cuisine.
